## What is Valstore?

Valstore is an open-source Flutter mobile app that gives you portable access to your Valorant in-game shop. Instead of launching the full game just to check your daily skin rotation, Valstore lets you view offers, browse the skin gallery, and manage a wishlist — all from your phone.

<Info>
  Valstore is an unofficial app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Riot Games. It uses Riot's public auth endpoints and the community-maintained [valorant-api.com](https://valorant-api.com) for skin data.
</Info>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in with your Riot account">
    Valstore opens Riot's official login page in an in-app browser. Your credentials never pass through Valstore — authentication happens directly with Riot's servers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your region">
    Choose your Valorant region (EU, NA, AP, KR, etc.) so the app connects to the correct server for your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse your shop">
    Your daily 4-skin offer rotates every ~24 hours. Valstore shows the skin name, cost in VP, content tier, and a countdown to the next refresh.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your wishlist">
    Add skins to your wishlist from the gallery. You'll receive a push notification the next time any wishlisted skin appears in your daily shop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Project status

<Warning>
  Valstore has been archived by its original author after being removed from Google Play at Riot's request. The source code remains available and the app continues to function. Feel free to fork and maintain your own build.
</Warning>

The project reached version **2.1.1+18** before archival and covers all major Valorant store features.
