Valstore is an open-source Flutter mobile app for Android that gives you portable access to your Valorant in-game shop. Instead of launching the full Valorant client to check your daily skin rotation, you can see your offers, browse the skin gallery, manage a wishlist, and receive push notifications — all from your phone.

It authenticates through Riot's official login endpoints and fetches skin data from the community-maintained [valorant-api.com](https://valorant-api.com). Valstore itself never handles your credentials; they go directly to Riot's servers.

The last released version is **2.1.1+18**.

## How authentication works

Valstore uses Riot's OAuth 2.0 authorization flow. When you log in, an in-app browser opens Riot's own login page at `auth.riotgames.com`. After you sign in, Riot redirects to a `playvalorant.com` URL that contains your access token. Valstore intercepts that redirect, extracts the token, and uses it to fetch your shop data from Riot's private API endpoints.

Your username and password are entered directly on Riot's page — Valstore never reads or stores them.

<Info>

</Info>
